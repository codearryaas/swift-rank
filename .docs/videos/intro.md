## üé® Asset Ideas & Requirements

### 1. Graphics & Icons
- **Rocket Icon**: To represent the "Swift" speed and boost.
- **Search Result Mockups**: Need high-quality PSD/Figma mockups of Google Search results to show "Before vs After" (Plain vs Rich Snippet).
- **5-Star Rating Animation**: Animated stars filling up from 0 to 5.
- **Tumbleweed GIF**: For the intro joke.

### 2. Screen Recordings
- **The Wizard**: Record the setup wizard flow. Speed it up 2x or 3x.
- **Settings Page**: Quick scan of the clean UI (highlighting 'Auto-detect' features).
- **Pro Features**: Briefly mouse-over some Pro toggles (teasing the premium power).

### 3. Audio / SFX
- **Background Music**: "Upbeat Corporate Funk" or "Tech House Lite".
- **SFX**: 
    - *Whoosh* (transitions)
    - *Ding!* (showing stars)
    - *Keyboard typing* (intro)
    - *Rocket launch* (logo reveal)

### 4. Color Palette
- **Primary**: Swift Rank Blue/Purple (Brand colors).
- **Accent**: Gold/Yellow (for Stars and Rankings).
- **Text**: White on Dark Background for high contrast overlays.

## üìù Key Features to Highlight (Cheat Sheet)
- **Zero Coding**: "If you can click a mouse, you can use this."
- **Templates & Conditions**: Total control over where your schema applies.
- **JSON-LD Schema**: The gold standard for SEO.
- **Rich Results**: Review stars, product info, video thumbs.
- **Local SEO**: crucial for physical stores.
- **Lightweight**: Doesn't slow down your site.
